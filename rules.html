Rules

  • Each player picks one Premier League team per week, using each team only once except for the two allowed twice.
  • Points are awarded for wins/draws and adjusted based on quartile differences.
  • Each player also picks a footballer each week; points awarded for goals & assists.
  • Leaderboard is updated weekly; remaining teams and cumulative points tracked.
  • End of season: player with most points wins.
  • Picks must be submitted before the weekly deadline.
